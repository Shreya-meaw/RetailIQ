# RetailIQ

##  Project Overview

The retailIQ Retail Sales Analytics System is a Business Intelligence project focused on analyzing retail sales data to uncover actionable business insights.

This project combines SQL, Data Warehousing concepts, Data Modeling, and Power BI to help businesses understand sales performance, customer behavior, product profitability, and operational challenges.

The goal is to transform raw sales data into meaningful dashboards and reports that support data-driven decision-making.

---

##  Project Objectives

- Analyze sales performance across products, customers, and locations.
- Identify top-performing and low-performing products.
- Detect loss-making orders and profitability issues.
- Measure the impact of discounts on profit.
- Analyze customer purchasing behavior and churn.
- Discover seasonal sales trends and revenue patterns.
- Build interactive dashboards for business stakeholders.
